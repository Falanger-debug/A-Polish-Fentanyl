The purpose

This website was created to raise public awareness about alcoholic products. Many people lack comprehensive information or base their knowledge on outdated research. My goal is not to persuade anyone to stop consuming alcohol. However, I aim to ensure that every decision is made consciously, with a full understanding of the potential risks involved.
